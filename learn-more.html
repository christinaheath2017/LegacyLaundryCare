How Legacy LaundryCare Works

It’s simple, flexible, and built for real life. You book online, I pick up your laundry, wash it with care, and return it folded and fresh. You’ll get a digital and paper receipt with final weight and itemized cost. No surprises, no stress.

Service Tiers

  • Standard: $40 minimum. Includes up to 20 lbs. $2.00/lb after that.
  • Express: $80 minimum. 12–18 hour turnaround. Rules apply.
  • Rapid Express: $120 minimum. 6–10 hour turnaround. Rules apply.
